I am an Associate Professor of Linguistics and Data Science at New York University, and a Research Scientist at Google. At NYU, I direct the Computation and Psycholinguistics Lab; we use behavioral experiments and computational methods to study how people learn and understand language. At both Google and NYU, I also work on large language model post-training, evaluation and interpretability.

I may be able to hire a post-doc in the coming months. Please reach out if interested!
